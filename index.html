Our Mission

You’ve decided to travel, but you have no idea where to sit back, relax, and have a beer on tap! Better yet, there’s a game this weekend – you can’t miss that. WHAT IF THERE WAS AN APPLICATION TO HELP YOU FIND BOTH? Using GitPub, by entering a city, you can find breweries near you. Feedback includes brewery name, type, address, phone number, and website! Broadcasted sporting events on the day that you are making the search can also be found – which breweries could be playing on the big screen. Of course, your search history will be saved, just in case.

Must be cities in U.S.
